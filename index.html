Systems

Empower workforce productivity, secure access to facilities and track employees in real-time.

Features
Real-time Performance Management

Employees can now connect through their Android/IOS Devices from any company access-point to track their attendance, working, and manage their profile.

Enterprise Security with RBAC

Execute your identity policy across all users, devices, cloud and on-premise applications using predefined job roles to control access.

Govern Employee Identity

Emply a real-time Identity Lifecycle Management and govern dynamic credentials and permissions granted to employees.

Open Integration Platform

Ease of integration with 3rd party application and internal systems.

Advanced Identity Analytics

This touchless SaaS platform allows live analytics, customized dashboards and identity access reports.

How it works?
Step 1 Generate QR code with employee or visitor registration
Step 2 QR codes are scanned at entrances.
Step 3 Platform validates employee role and access credential.
Step 4 Attendances are recorder and physical location occupancy can be monitored live via dashboards
Step 5 Custom reports generation for all employee attendance and facility access.
